Our Mission

Founded in 2024, HAHC creates platforms that support emerging artists while fostering accessibility, equity, and community engagement in the arts.

Who We Are

Located at 74 West 120th Street in Harlem, Harlem Art House Collective is a community-centered arts organization dedicated to transforming the cultural landscape. We believe that art is a powerful tool for social good and community building.

Our approach combines pop-up exhibitions, live performances, virtual auctions, and performance events that bring people together and celebrate the rich creative traditions of our community.
